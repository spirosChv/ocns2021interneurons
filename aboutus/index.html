Alexandra Tzilivaki (Primary Organizer)

Follow @TwitterDev 0000-0003-0140-2864

Einstein Center for Neurosciences (ECN), Charite Medical School, Berlin, Germany

Short bio

Alexandra Tzilivaki, received her BSc and MSc in Biology, with honors, from the University of Crete in Greece. She is currently a PhD candidate researcher in the laboratory of Prof. Dietmar Schmitz, PhD at Charite Medical School Berlin in Germany. She is also a member of Dr. Panayiota Poirazi's Laboratory in IMBB, FORTH in Greece. Alexandra is especially interested in understanding the contribution of interneurons in learning and memory processes with an emphasis to dendritic functions and hippocampal oscillations, via the utilization of modelling and experimental approaches. Her work has been supported among others by the Einstein Foundation, by the Onassis Foundation and by Google.

Short bio

Spyridon Chavlis, received his MEng from the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA), his MSc from Imperial College Londonand, and his PhD from the University of Crete in Computational Neuroscience under the supervision of Dr. P. Poirazi. He is currently a postdoctoral researcher in IMBB, FORTH in Crete, Greece. Siros is interested in understanding the role of large scale network dynamics in spatial memory encoding and in improving the current state-of-the-art Deep Learning architectures that incorporate rules inspired from the mammalian brain. His work has been supported among others by the Stavros Niarchos Foundation.
